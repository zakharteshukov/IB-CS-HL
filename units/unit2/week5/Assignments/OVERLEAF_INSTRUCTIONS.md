# How to Preview Your K-Map LaTeX on Overleaf

## Quick Steps:

1. **Go to Overleaf**: https://www.overleaf.com (already open in your browser)

2. **Sign up/Log in** (if needed):
   - Click "Register" or "Log in" 
   - You can use Google/GitHub to sign in quickly

3. **Create New Project**:
   - Click the green "New Project" button (top left)
   - Select "Blank Project"
   - Give it a name like "Karnaugh Map"

4. **Copy Your LaTeX Code**:
   - Open the file: `karnaugh_map.tex` in your editor
   - Select ALL (Cmd+A) and Copy (Cmd+C)
   - Go back to Overleaf
   - Delete the default template code
   - Paste your code (Cmd+V)

5. **View the PDF**:
   - The PDF preview appears automatically on the right side
   - If it doesn't compile, check the "Logs and output files" panel for errors
